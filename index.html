Hello, Myself Vishwa Patil!!

Animal Rescuer & Nature’s Friend

Want to know about me?!!

I'm Vishwa Patil, an animal lover and dedicated rescuer. My heart beats for every creature in need—whether it’s a bird with a broken wing, a puppy without shelter, or a baby animal in danger. Over the years, I've rescued and cared for sparrows, squirrels, dogs, cats, pigeons, and even chickens. Each life I've helped has left a pawprint on my heart.

Passion for Paws!!

Ever since I was a kid, I’ve had a deep love for dogs. Their loyalty and joy make every day brighter.

Rescue Mission!!

I dedicate my time to rescuing stray and abandoned dogs, giving them a second chance at life.

Forever Homes!!

Each rescued dog deserves a loving home. I help find safe and caring families for them.

Exploring the world "my love, my dogs!!"

My Rescue Stories

1.🦔 Squirrel Rescue

One of the most memorable moments was when I rescued a baby squirrel trapped inside a pipe. My friend and I carefully cut open the pipe and saved the little one. Although our families were initially upset about the chaos, they were proud of the compassion and courage we showed. We cared for the baby squirrel for a week, giving it food, warmth, and love until it was healthy again.

2.🐦 Sparrow Rescue

Sparrows hold a special place in my heart. I’ve healed about 4–5 baby sparrows that fell from their nests around my building. Many sparrows build nests in the windows, and often the little ones fall while trying to fly. I gently place them in a cozy box, apply turmeric for healing, and feed them until they are strong enough to fly again.

3.🐕‍ Puppy Rescue

I also once rescued a tiny white puppy injured by another dog. She was crying in pain on the road. I picked her up carefully and treated her wound. I wrapped her in a soft towel and kept her safe. After two weeks of love, food, and care, she became stronger and started playing again.

4.🐕 Dog Rescue

Dogs are a big part of my life. I’ve helped so many in my area that they now recognize me. Whether it's feeding them or simply being there when they’re hurt or scared, my bond with street dogs is something magical. Their wagging tails and loyal eyes are the most beautiful thank-you I could ever receive.

5.🐱 Cat Rescue

I once rescued a cat being chased by a dog. I brought her home and made sure she was safe. Later, I posted her story online, and her rightful owner found and thanked me. That cat taught me that sometimes, being kind just means stepping in at the right moment.

6.🦜 Parrot Rescue

One day, I found a parrot with a broken leg—he was scared, unable to fly, and in pain. I gently picked him up and brought him home, making sure he stayed calm and warm. We visited the vet, and I cared for him every day until his leg healed. Watching him finally spread his wings and fly away again was one of the most freeing, emotional moments of my life.

7.🕊️ Pigeon Rescue

A pigeon rescue happened when my friend accidentally startled a baby pigeon resting on his window. In fear, the bird jumped out, and we managed to rescue her just in time. She was terrified, but we helped her feel safe again.

8.🐔 Chicken Rescuee

My dad shares my love for animals. Once, he saw baby chickens being sold for meat and couldn’t bear it. He brought them home, and we raised them with care until they were old enough to live freely on our farm. That act of love still makes me smile.
